Welcome to the Kino Encyclopedia

Kino was more than a Soviet rock band — it was a cultural shockwave. Led by the iconic Viktor Tsoi , Kino blended post‑punk minimalism, poetic lyricism in suggestive topics of their song such as strong Anti-War message, corruption in modern society and struggle of a day to day citizen in the late Soviet-Era.

This website is planned to serve as archive and community forum dedicated to understanding the band’s history, their discography, personal stories, and their influence across generations.

Whether you're a longtime fan or someone discovering Kino for the first time, this space is here to guide you through their sound, their message, and their legacy.
